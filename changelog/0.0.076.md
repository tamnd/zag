- statistics: mean/fmean/median/median_low/median_high, mode/multimode,

  unescape covering `&lt;/&gt;/&amp;/&quot;/&apos;/&copy;/&hellip;` and
  numeric `&#NNN;` / `&#xHH;` refs.
